04 — About

Hi, I'm Krishiv.

I'm an Electronics & Communication Engineering student at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology (JIIT), drawn to real-life problems where software meets the physical world — traffic, pollution, drones, public infrastructure. I like bridging hardware and intelligent software, pairing agentic AI with a model of the real environment it has to act in.

More than anything, I love the zero-to-one of building startups — taking a rough idea, shipping it, and turning it into something people actually use. That drive is why I co-founded BrahmaMind, an early-stage deep-tech research initiative and the first venture I'm building toward. My flagship project, EcoShield Delhi, runs a fleet of water-sprinkler tankers over the real Delhi Metro map and routes them with a modified multi-objective Dijkstra algorithm.
